Conference Pricing

Interaction10 is over and we SOLD OUT! We thank every attendee for coming to Interaction10!

Conference Registration: $749 (early bird registration is over)
Student Registration: $299 (for students currently attending any college)

Standard and Student registrations included full admission to all conference sessions, beginning at 10am on Friday, February 5th and ending at 5:00pm on Sunday, February 7. They also included breakfast and lunch on Saturday and Sunday plus evening receptions on Friday and Saturday.

Balcony Pass: $99 (special pass for Savannah locals and students)

The Balcony Pass offers an easy way for local residents to attend Interaction10. It provides access to all sessions in the Trustees Theaters (including the keynote sessions) plus the Recruiting Fair and the Art Exhibition. However, it does not include access to sessions in the Old Pink House, Pharmacy, or Reynolds Square, nor any meals or evening receptions.

Pre-Conference Workshops

Individual Workshops: $250

Workshops are four hours long, taking place on Thursday, February 4, either in the morning (9am-1pm) or afternoon (2-6pm). Admission is limited to 45 participants per workshop.
