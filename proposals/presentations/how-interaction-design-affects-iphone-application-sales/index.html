How Interaction Design Affects iPhone Application Sales

Session Title

How Interaction Design Affects iPhone Application Sales

Presenter

Jen Gordon, Clever Twist, Inc.

Session Type: Presentation

Ideally this would be a presentation/discussion involving audience members that will shed light on their experience(s) with designing for iPhone, the nuances of the UI and how to creatively influence sales.

The App Store is in it’s infancy, but there’s a great deal of interest and total lack of discussion around what works and what doesn’t in iPhone interaction design. The purpose of this talk is to identify key elements that target user wants and ultimately result in high App Store rankings and/or sales.

Biography

Clever Twist is an Atlanta-based design studio focused on iPhone application design and development, with the goal of irradiating “ugly” from the App Store. “Designing iPhone apps the way Apple intended” is our mantra! Owner/Lead Juicer Jen Gordon brings 6 years of Art Direction/Design experience from a variety of corporate and small business clients, focusing on both brand identity and web design. www.whoisjengordon.com
