The behavior you’re seeing is the behavior you’ve designed for

Session Title

The behavior you’re seeing is the behavior you’ve designed for

Presenter

Joshua Porter, Bokardo Design

Session Type: Presentation

There is little accountability in design. When a design isn’t working, we rarely point the finger at anyone in particular.

But in a world of metrics and increasing fidelity in testing, interaction designers have an opportunity to seize responsibility for what happens on a web site or application. The question is: will they?

Biography

Joshua Porter is an interface designer and consultant. He wrote the book Designing for the Social Web and blogs at bokardo.com
