Proposals

Welcome to the public session submissions for Interaction10! We’re excited to let you view and comment on the submissions so we have a better idea of what type of content you would like to see. Your comments also allow proposer to clarify, tighten, and otherwise improve their submission, so ask questions, highlight gaps, suggest extensions or changes!

Selections will be curated by the conference committee using input from the public opinion. Our goal is a variety of session types, a variety of new and experienced interaction designers, and a variety of international voices.

Submissions and comments are now closed. It’s selection time! We’ll be announcing the full program soon.
